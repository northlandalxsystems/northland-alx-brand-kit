About Us

Northland ALX is a Pan-African IT consultancy and SaaS company based in Accra, Ghana. Our mission is to power Africa's digital transformation with reliable and scalable solutions.

Inside Northland ALX

Northland ALX is at the forefront of Africa’s digital revolution, delivering innovative solutions that bridge the gap between technology and real-world needs. Our portfolio spans gold traceability, agricultural compliance, digital finance, logistics, and education.

From GoldBod Systems—ensuring every gram of gold is tracked and monetized securely—to CTAP, Ghana’s unified student admissions platform, we build infrastructure that empowers people and organizations. Our IoT Compliance Modules and Agri/Cocoa Traceability platforms guarantee transparency and compliance in critical sectors.

We believe in a connected Africa, where technology is accessible, secure, and transformative. Our team combines deep technical expertise with a passion for impact, making Northland ALX the partner of choice for governments, enterprises, and communities across the continent.
